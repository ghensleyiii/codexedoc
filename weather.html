Weather Safety Tips

Stay safe with these weather tips: Check forecasts before outdoor activities, prepare for sudden changes, and use our radar to track approaching storms.

Read More

Preparing for Storms

Monitor radar for approaching storms and seek shelter if thunder is heard or lightning is seen. Avoid open fields or tall objects during thunderstorms.

Heat Safety

During high temperatures, stay hydrated, wear light clothing, and avoid strenuous activity during the hottest parts of the day. Check hourly forecasts to plan outdoor time.

Cold Weather Precautions

In cold weather, dress in layers, cover exposed skin, and check wind chill forecasts to prevent frostbite or hypothermia.

Flood Awareness

Avoid driving through flooded areas and monitor advisories for flash flood warnings. Use our weekly forecast to anticipate heavy rain periods.
