CODEXEDOC - Create With Code

Codexedoc is an online codecamp designed for people who want to start learning web development with a solid foundation. We provide beginner-friendly resources that focus on core concepts, practical exercises, and clear explanations—everything you need to start building confidently from the ground up.

I began coding at sixteen and quickly realized that finding the right starting point wasn't easy. Many tutorials jumped around, and some learning materials covered ideas that didn’t make sense until much later. I wanted a way to learn the essentials through actual practice—not just passively watching videos or reading pages of theory. That’s why I created Codexedoc.

This codecamp focuses on the foundational skills every web developer should understand and how everything fits together to create interactive websites. Every lesson is crafted to be clear, engaging, and paired with real coding projects that reinforce what you're learning. Step by step, you'll gain confidence and see tangible progress as you create things that actually work.

Codexedoc is ideal for beginners, career changers, or anyone curious about how websites are built. You don’t need prior experience—just a desire to learn and a willingness to try. We offer structure, guidance, and projects to help you stay on track and keep building. It’s all about starting with the right basics, practicing through projects, and growing your skills in a way that makes sense. Whether you're building your first webpage or trying to understand how developers build the sites you use every day, Codexedoc gives you the groundwork to create with code.
