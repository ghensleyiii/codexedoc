Building a Professional Portfolio and Resume

Mastering Your Portfolio and Resume

Welcome to Lesson 48! A professional portfolio and resume showcase your full-stack skills to employers. In this lesson, you’ll learn to create a portfolio website and resume, highlighting projects like your e-commerce platform.

What are Portfolios and Resumes?

A portfolio displays your projects with code and demos; a resume summarizes your skills and experience. In 2025, employers value deployed projects and clear, concise resumes for full-stack roles.

Key Concepts

Focus on these elements:

  • Portfolio Website: Build with React or HTML/CSS, hosted on GitHub Pages.
  • Project Descriptions: Explain tech stack, challenges, and solutions.
  • Resume Structure: Include skills, projects, and education.
  • GitHub Profile: Showcase clean, documented code.

Practical Applications

Your portfolio will showcase your capstone e-commerce project, making it a key asset for job applications and interviews.

Tips for Success

To build a strong portfolio and resume:

  • Highlight Projects: Include social media dashboard, inventory system, and e-commerce platform.
  • Keep It Concise: Limit resume to one page, portfolio to 3–5 projects.
  • Use GitHub: Link to clean, documented repositories.
  • Tailor Resume: Customize for each job role.

Summary

A professional portfolio and resume are your tickets to full-stack jobs. By showcasing your skills, you’re ready for the capstone project and final exam. Keep polishing your portfolio!
